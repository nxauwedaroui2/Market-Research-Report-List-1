<p><strong>The Electric Trains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"DC electrification system"</li><li>"AC electrification system"</li><li>"Composite system"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric trains market comprises three primary types of electrification systems. The DC electrification system typically operates at lower voltages and is commonly used in urban transit, providing efficient power over short distances. The AC electrification system, often utilized for long-distance and high-speed trains, operates at higher voltages, allowing for reduced transmission losses. The composite system integrates both AC and DC technologies, offering flexibility for varying rail networks and applications, enabling efficient power distribution tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
