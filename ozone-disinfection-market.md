<p><p>The ozone disinfection market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **High Pressure Discharge Ozone Disinfection** utilizes high voltage to generate ozone, offering rapid and efficient disinfection, particularly suitable for large-scale applications.</p><p>2. **Corona Discharge Ozone Disinfection** generates ozone by passing air through a high-voltage electric field, making it cost-effective for various water treatment processes.</p><p>3. **Photochemistry Ozone Disinfection** employs ultraviolet light to enhance ozone generation, providing a synergistic approach for disinfection, particularly in air and surface applications. Each method presents unique advantages based on specific disinfection needs.</p></p>
