<p><p>The thermostatic radiator valves (TRVs) market is primarily categorized into self-operating TRVs and electronic valves. Self-operating TRVs utilize a mechanical mechanism that adjusts the flow of hot water based on room temperature, providing a cost-effective and energy-efficient solution. In contrast, electronic valves offer enhanced control through programmable settings and sensors, allowing for precise temperature management. This differentiation caters to varying consumer preferences, with self-operating TRVs appealing to those seeking simplicity, while electronic valves attract users desiring advanced features and automation.</p></p>
