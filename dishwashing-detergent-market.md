<p><strong>The Dishwashing Detergent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saponification</li><li>Non-saponification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dishwashing detergent market can be categorized into two main types: saponification and non-saponification. Saponification involves the process where fats and oils react with an alkali, producing soap that effectively cleans dishes. Non-saponification encompasses synthetic detergents, which are formulated with surfactants that don't rely on traditional soap-making processes. Both types serve to break down grease and food residues, but cater to varying consumer preferences regarding eco-friendliness and cleaning efficiency.</p></p>
