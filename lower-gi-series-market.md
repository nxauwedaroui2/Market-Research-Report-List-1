<p><p>The Lower GI Series market is segmented into two main types: Double-Contrast and Single-Contrast. Double-Contrast utilizes a combination of barium sulfate and air to provide enhanced visualization of the intestinal lining, allowing for detailed assessment of abnormalities. In contrast, Single-Contrast involves the use of barium sulfate alone, offering a simpler imaging technique but with less detail. Both methods are essential in diagnosing gastrointestinal conditions, catering to different diagnostic needs and preferences in clinical practice.</p></p>
