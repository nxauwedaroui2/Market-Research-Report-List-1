<p><p>The Copper Pillar Flip Chip market comprises three main IC types: 3D IC, 2.5D IC, and 2D IC. 3D IC technology stacks multiple chips vertically, enhancing performance and reducing footprint, ideal for space-constrained applications. 2.5D IC uses an interposer to connect multiple chiplets, facilitating improved speed and bandwidth while maintaining a flat layout. In contrast, 2D IC integrates components side-by-side on a single substrate, focusing on simplicity and cost-efficiency, suitable for traditional applications. Each type offers distinct advantages for various electronic devices.</p></p>
