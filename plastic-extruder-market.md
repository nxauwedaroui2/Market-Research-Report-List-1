<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plastic Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Single-screw</li><li>Double-screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic extruder market is categorized into various types based on design and functionality. Plunger extruders utilize a piston to push material through the die, ideal for high-viscosity applications. Single-screw extruders are the most common, featuring a single rotating screw that melts and homogenizes plastic. Double-screw extruders, with two intermeshing screws, enhance mixing and output, suitable for compound production. Multi-screw extruders employ multiple screws to further improve mixing efficiency and process flexibility, used in specialized applications.</p></p>
