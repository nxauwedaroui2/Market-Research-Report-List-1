<p><p>The HPV diagnostic product market encompasses various testing methodologies, including In Situ Hybridization, HC2 Technology, and PCR. In Situ Hybridization identifies specific nucleic acid sequences within tissue samples, allowing for direct visualization of HPV. HC2 Technology utilizes a hybrid capture process to detect high-risk HPV types via amplified signals. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) offers rapid and sensitive detection of HPV DNA by amplifying target sequences. Each method provides distinct advantages for accurate HPV diagnosis and patient management.</p></p>
