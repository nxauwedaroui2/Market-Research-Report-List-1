<p><strong>The Industrial DTG Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet (CIJ)</li><li>Drop-on-Demand (DOD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial DTG (Direct-to-Garment) Printer market features two main types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-on-Demand (DOD). CIJ technology constantly circulates ink, allowing for high-speed printing on various materials, making it ideal for large production runs. Conversely, DOD technology releases ink droplets only when required, providing greater precision and detail, which is beneficial for intricate designs and shorter runs. Both technologies cater to different production needs, enhancing versatility in the textile printing industry.</p></p>
