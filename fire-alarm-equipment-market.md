<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>Addressable Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire alarm equipment market is primarily divided into two types: conventional and addressable systems. Conventional systems utilize a series of detectors and manual call points connected to a control panel, providing basic zone identification during an alarm. In contrast, addressable systems employ intelligent technology, allowing each device to have a unique address, enabling precise location identification and quicker response times. This advanced capability enhances fault detection and monitoring, making addressable systems increasingly popular in complex environments.</p></p>
