<p><p>Scaffolding market types include Frame Scaffolding, Fastener Scaffold, Bowl-buckle Scaffold, and others. Frame Scaffolding consists of prefabricated frames providing quick assembly and stability for construction projects. Fastener Scaffolds utilize bolts and clamps for secure connections, offering flexibility in design. Bowl-buckle Scaffolds feature a unique bowl-and-pin system for easy assembly and strong load-bearing capabilities. Other types encompass variations like modular and suspended scaffolding, catering to specific project needs and enhancing safety and efficiency in various construction environments.</p></p>
