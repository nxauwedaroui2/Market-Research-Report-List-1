<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) is a technology used in manufacturing to detect defects in products by analyzing images captured of the items. The AOI market is categorized into two types: inline and offline. Inline AOI systems are integrated within the production line, allowing real-time inspections to catch defects immediately, thereby enhancing efficiency. Offline AOI systems, on the other hand, operate separately from the production line, enabling detailed inspections after manufacturing, which can benefit quality control processes and product validation.</p></p>
