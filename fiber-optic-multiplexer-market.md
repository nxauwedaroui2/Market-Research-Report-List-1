<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Multiplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM</li><li>WDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic multiplexer market primarily includes two types: Frequency Division Multiplexing (FDM) and Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM). FDM combines multiple signals into a single optical fiber by assigning different frequency bands to each signal, optimizing bandwidth usage. In contrast, WDM uses multiple wavelengths on a single fiber, enabling simultaneous data transmission across distinct channels. Both technologies enhance data capacity and efficiency, catering to the growing demand for high-speed communication in telecommunications and data networking.</p></p>
