<p><p>The sound level calibrator market is divided into Class I and Class II calibrators. Class I sound calibrators are designed for high-precision measurements, suitable for laboratory and research environments, ensuring accuracy within ±0.2 dB. Class II sound calibrators are more versatile and cost-effective, typically used in field measurements and less critical applications, with an accuracy within ±0.5 dB. Both types serve essential roles in verifying the performance of sound level meters and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
