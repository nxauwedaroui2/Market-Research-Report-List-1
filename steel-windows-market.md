<p><p>The steel windows market is divided into two primary types: closed type and openable type. Closed type windows are fixed installations that provide durability and security while offering minimal ventilation. They are ideal for structures requiring maximum thermal and acoustic insulation. In contrast, openable type windows feature operational mechanisms allowing ventilation, enhancing airflow and light without sacrificing security. Each type serves distinct architectural needs, catering to preferences for aesthetics, functionality, and environmental control in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
