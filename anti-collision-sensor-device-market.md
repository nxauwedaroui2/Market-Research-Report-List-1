<p><p>The anti-collision sensor device market comprises several key technologies, including radar, cameras, ultrasound, and LiDAR. Radar systems use radio waves to detect objects and measure distances, offering reliability in diverse weather conditions. Cameras capture visual data for object recognition and classification, but may struggle in low visibility. Ultrasound sensors measure distance through sound waves, suitable for short-range applications. LiDAR employs laser pulses to create detailed 3D maps, providing high accuracy and precision in detecting surroundings, enhancing safety in various applications.</p></p>
