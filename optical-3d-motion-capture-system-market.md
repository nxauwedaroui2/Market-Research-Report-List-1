<p><p>The Optical 3D Motion Capture System market is primarily divided into two types: Active and Passive. Active systems utilize electronic markers that emit their own light, enabling precise tracking in real-time and often providing higher accuracy and versatility for dynamic movements. In contrast, Passive systems use reflective markers that rely on external light sources, making them generally more cost-effective and simpler to set up. Both types are widely used in various fields such as film production, gaming, and biomechanics.</p></p>
