<p><ul><li>LOX</li><li>OBOGS</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Onboard Oxygen Systems market is categorized into three main types: Liquid Oxygen (LOX), On-Board Oxygen Generating Systems (OBOGS), and others. LOX systems utilize liquid oxygen stored at low temperatures, providing high-density oxygen for high-altitude applications. OBOGS generates oxygen from the ambient air, ensuring a continuous supply during flight. Other systems may include hybrid solutions or advanced technologies tailored for specific aircraft requirements. These systems are crucial for ensuring crew safety and performance in various aerospace environments.</p></p>
