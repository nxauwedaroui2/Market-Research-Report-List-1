<p><ul><li>Full Truckload</li><li>Less-Than-Truckload</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road freight transportation market can be categorized into Full Truckload (FTL) and Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) segments. FTL involves transporting a single shipment that occupies the entire truck, making it ideal for large volumes or long distances. In contrast, LTL consolidates multiple smaller shipments from different customers within the same truck, optimizing transport costs for goods that do not require full truck capacity. Each type serves distinct logistical needs, balancing efficiency and cost for shippers.</p></p>
