<p><p>The keyboard stabilizer market consists of three main types: screw-in, snap-in, and plate-mounted stabilizers. Snap-in stabilizers are easy to install and often found in pre-built keyboards, providing a user-friendly option. Plate-mounted stabilizers are integrated into the keyboard plate, enhancing durability and stability. Each type caters to different preferences and keyboard setups, influencing performance and user satisfaction.</p></p>
