<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic</li><li>Hydrophilic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fumed alumina, a versatile material used in various applications, is categorized into two primary market types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic. Hydrophobic fumed alumina is treated to repel water, making it ideal for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as coatings and sealants. In contrast, hydrophilic fumed alumina readily absorbs moisture, which is beneficial in products like adhesives and inks where improved dispersion and adhesion are needed. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, enhancing product performance and stability.</p></p>
