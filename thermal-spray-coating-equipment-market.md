<p><p>Thermal spray coating equipment encompasses various techniques used to apply protective coatings on surfaces. Plasma spray utilizes a high-temperature plasma arc to melt feedstock materials, resulting in strong coatings. High-velocity oxy-fuel (HVOF) spray achieves high-speed particle impact for dense coatings, enhancing durability. Arc spray employs an electric arc to melt metal wires, providing coatings with good adhesion. Other market types include wire flame spray and cold spray, each offering unique properties for specific industrial applications and material enhancements.</p></p>
