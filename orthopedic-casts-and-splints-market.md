<p><p>The orthopedic casts and splints market is divided into two main categories: casts and splints. Casts are rigid supports used to immobilize broken bones and allow for proper healing, commonly made from materials like fiberglass or plaster. Splints, on the other hand, provide temporary support and stabilization to injured areas, being more flexible and adjustable than casts. Both are integral in orthopedic treatment, catering to varying injury types, patient needs, and recovery processes.</p></p>
