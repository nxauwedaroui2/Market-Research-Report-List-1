<p><p>The home ventilator market is primarily categorized into three types: Single-Level Ventilators, Bi-Level Ventilators, and Others. Single-Level Ventilators provide continuous airflow at a fixed pressure, making them suitable for patients with uncomplicated respiratory needs. Bi-Level Ventilators offer two distinct pressure levels—one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation, catering to more complex respiratory conditions. The "Others" category includes specialized or advanced ventilation devices that may integrate additional features, enhancing patient comfort and care tailored to individual requirements.</p></p>
