<p><p>Microphone phantom adapters are devices used to provide phantom power to condenser microphones, which require it for optimal performance. The market is primarily divided into two types: in-line and embedded. In-line adapters are standalone units that connect between the microphone and the audio interface, offering flexibility and ease of use. Embedded adapters, on the other hand, are integrated directly into audio devices or mixers, streamlining the setup and reducing clutter. Both types enhance audio quality by supplying consistent power.</p></p>
