<p><p>Amalgam carriers are dental instruments used to transport and place dental amalgam material. There are two primary market types: single and double amalgam carriers. Single carriers hold and deliver one specific amount of amalgam, making them ideal for precise fillings. Double carriers, on the other hand, can hold two different amounts of amalgam, allowing for versatility in treatment options and improved efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in dental procedures, enhancing the accuracy and effectiveness of amalgam placement.</p></p>
