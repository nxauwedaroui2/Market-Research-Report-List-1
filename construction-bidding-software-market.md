<p><p>Construction bidding software can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers and accessed via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT overhead. This model allows for real-time collaboration and updates, making it increasingly popular among construction firms seeking efficiency and mobility in their bidding processes.</p></p>
