<p><p>Forging presses are categorized primarily into mechanical and hydraulic types, each serving distinct applications in metal shaping. Mechanical forging presses utilize a system of gears and levers to deliver a rapid and powerful stroke, ideal for high-speed production and consistent part dimensions. In contrast, hydraulic forging presses use hydraulic fluid to generate compressive force, allowing for greater control and flexibility in shaping complex or intricate designs. These differences make each type suitable for various manufacturing needs and materials.</p></p>
