<p><p>Fiber Viable Bone Matrix (FVBM) is an innovative biomaterial used in orthopedic and dental surgeries to enhance bone regeneration. Composed of a complex network of collagen fibers combined with viable osteogenic cells, FVBM supports the body's natural healing processes. Its unique structure facilitates the integration of new bone growth, making it an essential component in procedures like spinal fusion, fracture repair, and dental implants.</p><p>The Fiber Viable Bone Matrix Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing prevalence of orthopedic disorders, advancements in regenerative medicine, and rising consumer awareness about minimally invasive surgical techniques. Moreover, the expanding elderly population, who are more prone to bone-related issues, is expected to drive demand for FVBM products. </p><p>Recent trends in the market highlight a growing inclination towards bioactive materials that not only provide structural support but also promote cellular activities critical for bone healing.
