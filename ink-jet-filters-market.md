<p><p>The ink-jet filters market is segmented into three main types based on ink formulations: pigment-based inks, dye-based inks, and solvent-based inks. Pigment-based inks offer superior lightfastness and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Dye-based inks provide vibrant colors but have lower longevity, ideal for indoor use. Solvent-based inks are used for industrial and outdoor purposes due to their resistance to water and UV light. Each type caters to specific printing needs and environments, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
