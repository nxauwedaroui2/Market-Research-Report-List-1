<p><p>The food safety testing kit market comprises several types, including PCR-based kits, immunoassay-based kits, and others. PCR-based kits utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to detect specific DNA sequences, enabling rapid identification of pathogens and contaminants. Immunoassay-based kits rely on antibody-antigen interactions to identify pathogens or toxins, providing results quickly and efficiently. Other methods may include traditional culture techniques or bio-sensor technologies. Together, these types ensure food safety by enabling accurate and timely detection of harmful substances.</p></p>
