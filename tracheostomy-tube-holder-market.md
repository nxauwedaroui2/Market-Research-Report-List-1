<p><p>The tracheostomy tube holder market features two main designs: two-piece and one-piece. In contrast, one-piece designs integrate the holder and strap into a single unit, simplifying application and reducing potential points of failure. Both designs aim to provide secure, comfortable support for tracheostomy tubes, catering to varying patient needs and preferences in respiratory care.</p></p>
