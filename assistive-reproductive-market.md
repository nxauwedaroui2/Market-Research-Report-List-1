<p><p>The assistive reproductive market includes various types of procedures designed to aid conception. In-vitro fertilization (IVF) involves extracting eggs and fertilizing them outside the body before implanting them in the uterus. Artificial insemination, specifically intrauterine insemination (AI-IUI), involves placing sperm directly into the uterus to enhance the chances of fertilization. Frozen embryo replacement (FER) allows the use of previously frozen embryos for implantation. Additionally, other market types encompass a range of alternative reproductive technologies and fertility treatments.</p></p>
