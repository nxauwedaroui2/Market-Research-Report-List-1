<p><p>The Bleached Non-Absorbent Cotton Wool market is primarily categorized into two types: Virgin Cotton and Noil. Virgin Cotton refers to high-quality, unprocessed cotton fibers that are chemically treated and bleached for enhanced purity and softness, making it ideal for medical and cosmetic applications. In contrast, Noil consists of shorter, lower-quality cotton fibers that are typically removed during the spinning process. Though less refined, Noil is often utilized in industrial applications and for economical purposes.</p></p>
