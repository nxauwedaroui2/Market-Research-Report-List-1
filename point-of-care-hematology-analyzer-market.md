<p><ul><li>3-Part Differentiation</li><li>5-Part Differentiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Point of Care Hematology Analyzers are classified into two main types: 3-Part Differentiation and 5-Part Differentiation. </p><p>3-Part Differentiation analyzers measure three main components: red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelet counts. They provide a basic overview, suitable for quick assessments. </p><p>In contrast, 5-Part Differentiation analyzers offer a more detailed analysis by categorizing white blood cells into five distinct types. This advanced profiling enhances diagnostic accuracy and informs treatment decisions, making them ideal for comprehensive patient evaluations.</p></p>
