<p><p>The Automotive Battery Thermal Management System market is segmented into liquid and air cooling/heating systems. Liquid cooling and heating systems utilize coolant to effectively manage battery temperatures, enhancing performance and lifespan. In contrast, air cooling and heating systems rely on airflow to regulate temperature, offering a simpler design but potentially less efficiency. Each type presents unique advantages, with liquid systems typically providing better temperature control, while air systems are more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both are essential for optimizing battery performance in electric vehicles.</p></p>
