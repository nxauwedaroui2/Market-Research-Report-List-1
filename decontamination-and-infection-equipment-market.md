<p><p>The Decontamination and Infection Equipment Market includes Chamber Decontamination and Room Decontamination types. Chamber Decontamination refers to enclosed systems designed to sterilize specific items or materials using methods like ethylene oxide, hydrogen peroxide, or ozone. In contrast, Room Decontamination focuses on larger spaces, utilizing technologies such as ultraviolet light or vaporized hydrogen peroxide to ensure entire environments are free from pathogens. Both methods are essential in healthcare settings to prevent infection and maintain safety standards.</p></p>
