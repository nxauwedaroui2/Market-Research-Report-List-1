<p><p>The 3D Laser Sorting Machine market features two primary types: Belt Sorting Machines and Rotary Disc Sorting Machines. Belt Sorting Machines utilize a conveyor belt system to transport materials while employing laser technology to identify and differentiate items, making them ideal for high-speed sorting. In contrast, Rotary Disc Sorting Machines use rotating discs to separate materials based on their size and shape, offering precision and versatility for diverse applications. Both types enhance sorting efficiency in industries like recycling, manufacturing, and food processing.</p></p>
