<p><p>Multiphase pump systems are essential for efficiently transporting mixtures of gas, liquid, and solids in various industries. The Twin Screw Type utilizes two intermeshing screws to create a continuous flow, ideal for handling viscous fluids. The Helico-Axial Type employs a helical design to move fluids axially, providing high flow rates and efficiency. "Others" encompass various alternative designs, including centrifugal and diaphragm pumps, each tailored for specific applications and fluid characteristics, enhancing flexibility in multiphase transport solutions.</p></p>
